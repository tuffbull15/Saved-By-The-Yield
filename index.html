Rocket ETH Strategy

The 4-1-1

rETH is Rocket Pool's liquid staked ETH. Similar to Lido Finance, when you deposit your ETH into Rocket Pool, you get rETH in return. The ETH is then staked with Rocket Pool's validators and you reap the benefits of that yield.

There is one big difference between Lido's stETH and Rocket Pool's rETH and that's the way the token "rebases". Instead of your rETH balance increasing every day, the amount of ETH you can swap your rETH for gradually increases over time. This makes it more tax-efficient than it's Lido counterpart.

Simply put, hold rETH and watch it become more valuable over time!

Defi Tools

These are some of my favorite tools when it comes to managing my defi positions.

Wallets

Hardware

First off, hardware wallets are basically the safest way to store your crypto. Before you even think about utilizing any of the eth strategies on this page, you NEED to get a hardware wallet. Trust me, all the cool kids are doing it.

Now, when it comes to hardware wallets, it mostly stops and ends with Ledger or Trezor. I personally have both, but, when it comes to defi, my Ledger Nano x is the bees-knees.

Don't ask me what bees-knees are. I have no idea.

Also, whether you land on a Ledger or a Trezor or any other hardware wallet, I always recommend buying them directly from their respective websites to ensure they haven't been compromised.

Hot Wallets

A "hot" wallet for all you n00bs out there, is a crypto wallet that is basically connected to the internet. These are mostly mobile or browser extension wallets and, for obvious reasons, are less secure than their hardware comrades.

People love them though because they're a lot easier to use and make interacting with dApps a breeze.

The favorite, by far, is MetaMask. Everyone and their favorite crypto degen uses it, including yours truly. (But don't get it twisted, most of my defi is done by connecting my secure hardware wallet to my MetaMask.)

Coinbase also has their own hot wallet that I've used and it definitely gets the job done.

In the end, play around and choose the one you like. Crypto is all about exploring this new digital frontier!

Defi Tracking Protocols

Zapper Finance

Zapper is "an asset management platform for Web3." Basically, you put in your crypto address and it helps track all of your assets and defi positions across multiple chains in one easy dashboard.

Trust me, when you really start yolo'ing into various defi strategies, this thing becomes an absolute lifesaver.

Debank

DeBank is basically the same thing as Zapper. It's a defi portfolio tracker that helps you track all of your assets across various blockchains.

Again, these things are a total life-saver. Play around and find out which one works best for you!
